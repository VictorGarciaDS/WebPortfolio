The calibration estimator

Calibration is the re-weighting that is used when we have variables (qualitative or quantitative) on which we want to jointly adjust. Such calibrations will provide unbiased estimates consistents with the design and will have a mean square error smaller than the of the Horvitz-Thompson estimator.
